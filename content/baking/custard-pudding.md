# Custard Pudding
## Ingredienten (Ingredients):
- 1 liter milk (4 cups)
- 60g cornstarch / maizena (4 tbsp)
- 45g sugar / suiker (3 tbsp)
- 5ml (1 tsp) vanilla extract / vanille extract
- Pinch of salt / snufje zout

## Instructies (Instructions):
1. Heat 800ml (3.5 cups) milk in a saucepan with vanilla and sugar over medium heat
2. In a small bowl, mix 200ml (1 cup) cold milk with cornstarch until smooth paste forms
3. When the milk in the pan is almost boiling, reduce heat to low
4. Slowly pour in the cornstarch mixture while stirring constantly
5. Continue stirring for 2-3 minutes until the pudding thickens
6. Remove from heat and pour into 6 serving bowls
7. Let cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours
8. Serve cold, optionally with fruit or whipped cream
