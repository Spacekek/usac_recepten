## Ingredients:
- 200g tortilla chips
- 400g black beans (1 can), drained
- 150g shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
- 100g sour cream (7 tbsp)
- 120ml (1/2 cup) salsa
- 30g (2 tbsp) jalapeños, sliced (optional)
- 1 ripe avocado, diced
- 10g (2 tbsp) fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1 lime, cut into wedges

## Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F)
2. Spread tortilla chips evenly on a baking sheet
3. Distribute black beans over the chips
4. Sprinkle shredded cheese evenly on top
5. Bake for 10-15 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly
6. Remove from oven and top with dollops of sour cream
7. Add salsa, diced avocado, jalapeños, and cilantro
8. Serve immediately with lime wedges
