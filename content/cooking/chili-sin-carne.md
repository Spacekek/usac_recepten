## Ingredients
- 2 cans (800g total) kidney beans, drained
- 400g crushed tomatoes (1 can)
- 200g corn (1 can), drained
- 2 medium onions, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 15ml (1 tbsp) olive oil
- 5g (1 tsp) cumin
- 2.5g (1/2 tsp) chili powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat
2. Add diced onion and cook until translucent (5-7 minutes)
3. Add minced garlic and spices, cook for 1 minute until fragrant
4. Add crushed tomatoes, beans, and corn
5. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste
7. Serve hot with rice or tortilla chips
