## Ingredients
- 400g risotto rice
- 1 medium (~500g) butternut squash
- 1 onion
- 3 cloves of garlic
- 150g grana padano cheese (or Parmesan)
- 25g butter
- 3g dried sage
- 3g black pepper
- 1.5l mushroom stock (3 stock cubes with 1.5l water)
- 20ml olive oil
- (Optional 100ml of white wine)

## Instructions
### Prep:
1. Dice the onion
2. Press the garlic (or cut into small pieces)
3. Peel and Cut the pumpkin into small cubes
4. Dissolve the stock cubes in hot water
5. Grate the cheese

### Cooking:
1. Saute the onion on low-medium heat in a large pot with some olive oil until translucent
2. Add the garlic
3. After 1 min add the pumpkin and risotto rice
4. Toast the rice 1-2 min until the edges become translucent, then de-glaze with a little bit of stock (or optionally white wine) ~100ml
5. Add stock whenever it starts to become dry and mix in sage and pepper
6. When rice is done (check by taste), turn off the heat and mix in the cheese and butter
7. Serve and enjoy!
