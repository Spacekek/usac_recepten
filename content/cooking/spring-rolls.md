## Ingredients

Half everything if dish is a starter instead of a main course

* Rice paper sheets 40
* Rice vermicelli / mihoen (350 g)
* Carrot (400 g)
* Cucumber (1 piece)
* Bell peppers (4 pieces)
* Lettuce (1 head)
* Mint (1 packet)
* Egg / smoked tofu (veg/vegan, 10 eggs / 500 g)
* Red cabbage or regular cabbage (600 g or 1 head)
* Bean sprouts (250 g)

Peanut sauce:

* Peanut butter (10 tbsp)
* Sambal (5 tsp)
* Soy sauce (5 tbsp)
* Vinegar (5 tsp)
* (Optional: extra chili flakes and a few drops of sesame oil)
* Water until it becomes a sauce

## Instructions

1. Slice vegetables into thin slices
2. Bake egg/tofu
3. Heat all peanut sauce ingredients in a small sauce pan
4. Cook vermicelli for the number of minutes specified on the packaging
5. Dip rice paper in water, add a small amount of all the ingredients and close:
