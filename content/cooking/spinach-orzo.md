# Spinach Orzo
## Ingredients:
- Spinach
- Mushrooms
- Mascarpone or heavy cream
- Orzo
- Lemon Juice

## Instructions:
1. Fry spinach and mushrooms
2. Add orzo
3. Add stock every time the bottom is dry
4. Add Mascarpone and lemon juice
