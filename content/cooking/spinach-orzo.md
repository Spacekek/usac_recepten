# Spinach Orzo
## Ingredients:
- 300g orzo pasta
- 200g fresh spinach
- 250g mushrooms, sliced (white or cremini)
- 100g mascarpone or heavy cream (7 tbsp)
- 750ml (3 cups) vegetable stock
- 30ml (2 tbsp) lemon juice (about 1/2 lemon)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 15ml (1 tbsp) olive oil
- 50g parmesan cheese, grated (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat
2. Add minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant
3. Add sliced mushrooms and cook for 5-7 minutes until golden brown
4. Add fresh spinach and cook until wilted (2-3 minutes)
5. Add orzo pasta and stir to coat with the vegetables
6. Add vegetable stock gradually (about 250ml at a time), stirring frequently like risotto
7. Continue adding stock and stirring until orzo is al dente (15-18 minutes total)
8. Stir in mascarpone and lemon juice until creamy
9. Season with salt and pepper to taste
10. Top with grated parmesan if desired and serve hot
